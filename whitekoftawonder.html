White kofta wonder

Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken cubes 500g
  • 1 medium onion
  • 2-3 Green chillies
  • Fresh coriander chopped 2 tbs
  • Ginger garlic paste 1 tsp
  • Cumin powder 1 tsp
  • Himalayan pink salt 1⁄2 tsp or to taste
  • Black pepper powder 1⁄2 tsp
  • Red chilli crushed 1 tsp
  • Garam masala powder 1⁄2 tsp
  • Clarified butter 1 & 2 tbs
  • Bread slice 1
  • Cooking oil 5-6 tbs
  • Pyaz (Onion) roughly sliced 3-4 small
  • Hari elaichi (Green cardamom) 3-4
  • Hari mirch (Green chillies) 4-5
  • Badam (Almonds) soaked & peeled 8-9
  • Char maghaz (Melon seeds) 2 tbs
  • Water 3-4 tbs
  • Cream 4 Cup
  • Dried fenugreek leaves 1 tsp
  • Fresh coriander chopped

Instructions:

  1. In a chopper,add chicken,onion,green chillies,fresh coriander,ginger garlic paste,cumin powder,pink salt, black pepper powder,red chilli crushed,garam masala powder,clarified butter,bread slice & chop until well combined.
  2. Grease hands with oil,take a small quantity of mixture (50g) & make koftay of equal sizes.
  3. In a wok,add cooking oil,prepared chicken koftay & fry on low flame from all sides until light golden & set aside (makes 12).
  4. In the same wok,add onion,green cardamom & fry on medium flame for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Take out onion & transfer to a blending jar,add green chillies,almonds,melon seeds,water & blend well.-In the same wok,add blended paste & mix well.
  6. Add black pepper powder,cumin powder,mace powder,coriander powder,garam masala powder,pink salt,ginger garlic paste,yogurt & mix well,cover & cook on low flame for 4-5 minutes.
  7. Add water,mix well & cook on medium flame for 1-2 minutes.
  8. Turn off the flame,add cream,dried fenugreek leaves & mix well.
  9. Turn on the flame,add prepared fried koftay & mix gently.
  10. Add fresh coriander,cover & cook on low flame for 4-5 minutes.
  11. Serve with naan or chapati!
