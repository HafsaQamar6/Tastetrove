Toast with avocado and poached egg

Ingredients:

  • Two pieces of whole grain bread
  • One mature avocado
  • two eggs
  • To taste, add salt and pepper.
  • Feta cheese, cherry tomatoes, and red pepper flakes are optional garnishes.

Instructions:

  1. Bread pieces should be toasted till golden brown.
  2. In a bowl, mash the ripe avocado and add salt and pepper to taste.
  3. In boiling water, poach the eggs for 3â€“4 minutes, or until the yolks are still runny but the whites are set.
  4. Evenly distribute the mashed avocado over the bread slices.
  5. On each toast, place a poached egg.
  6. Top with any optional toppings you choose.
  7. Serve right away.
