Tikki Toastie Treat

Ingredients:

  • 2 Bread Pieces
  • 1/4 Cup Water
  • 2 Boiled Egg
  • 1⁄2 Tspn Chilli Flakes
  • 1⁄2 Tspn Salt
  • 1⁄2 Tspn Coriander Powder
  • 14 Tspn Ginger
  • 14 Tspn Garlic
  • 1⁄2 Tspn Cumin
  • 1 Tbsp Onion
  • 2 Green Chilli
  • 1 Tbsp Coriander Leaves
  • 2 Tbsp Cornflour

Instructions:

  1. Squeeze out the water.
  2. Grate the eggs.
  3. take a bowl.
  4. Add all the Ingredients.
  5. Mix Well.
  6. Grease Oil in Hand.
  7. Make Shape.
  8. Coat with Breadcrumb.
  9. Fry in Medium Heated Oil Fry For 5-7 Min.
