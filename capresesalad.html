Caprese Salad

Ingredients:

  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Fresh Mozzarella Cheese
  • Fresh Basil Leaves
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar
  • Salt and Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Slice the tomatoes and mozzarella cheese into even, thick slices.
  2. Arrange tomato, mozzarella, and basil leaves on a serving platter, alternating them.
  3. Drizzle extra virgin olive oil and balsamic vinegar over the salad.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve immediately and enjoy!
