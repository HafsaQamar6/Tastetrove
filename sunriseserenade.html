Sunrise Serenade

Ingredients:

  • Eggs 2
  • salt 1/4 tsp or to taste
  • butter as required
  • cheese slices or mozzarella cheese grated
  • Tikka Mayonnaise

Instructions:

  1. take a bowl.
  2. add 2 eggs and salt.
  3. mix the mixture well.
  4. take a slice of bread and cut it from middle.
  5. take a pan and garnish it with butter.
  6. add egg mixture and place bread pieces on top of it.
  7. cook it for 2 minutes and then filp it.
  8. add cheese slice and tikka mayo on top of it.
  9. fold it and cook its both sides for 2 minutes.
  10. Serve it and enjoy it!
